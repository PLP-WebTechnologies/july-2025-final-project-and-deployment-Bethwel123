About This Educational Resource

This website was created to help Form 3 Physics students understand Newton's Laws of Motion in an engaging and interactive way.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand and explain Newton's three laws of motion
  • Apply the laws to real-world situations
  • Solve problems using F=ma
  • Identify action-reaction pairs

Features:

  • Clear explanations with diagrams
  • Interactive demonstrations
  • Practical examples
  • Self-assessment quiz

This resource aligns with the Kenyan secondary school physics curriculum.
